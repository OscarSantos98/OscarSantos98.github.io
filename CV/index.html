November 2019 - November 2019

Summer of Research

Determination of thermal efficiency of solar heater prototypes

The performance of a solar heater with a parallel pipe array was compared to a heater with a spiral pipe array designed with unconventional materials. The system was monitored by a remote measurement system with Arduino through a Bluetooth module to measure the temperature in both heaters with DS18B20 sensors. The temperatures were measured every hour from 8:00 to 18:00 and their performance was evaluated with Matlab.
